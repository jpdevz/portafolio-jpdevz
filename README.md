# JPDEVZ Portfolio

Personal portfolio showcasing how I design digital solutions that simplify business processes through web applications, automation and artificial intelligence.

## Overview

This portfolio reflects my approach to software development:

- Understand the business first.
- Identify repetitive processes and operational bottlenecks.
- Design digital solutions that generate real value.

Rather than focusing on technologies, the portfolio focuses on solving business problems.
